My Services

I dedicate myself to ensuring that I produce a project that not only meets but exceeds your starting vision. In order to do that it’s important you know what you can expect:

  1. Consultation - This is the part of the process that I enjoy. I discover what your vision is and how I can help.
  2. Proposal - After meeting a detailed proposal will be presented and a plan of action is decided.
  3. Creation - The magic starts! I start building out your project and you receive regular updates about the project while I create and test.
  4. Launch - The culmination of your vision and my creation collide and the project is launched.
  5. Support - Peace of mind is offered in the form of optional ongoing support.
